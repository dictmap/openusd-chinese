原文摘录 / English Source Excerpts

Defines the properties that enable audio playback in the scene.

SpatialAudio specifies which audio should play ( filePath ), the mode in which the audio is played ( auralMode ), and the playback settings (combinations of startTime , endTime , playbackMode , and mediaOffset ).
