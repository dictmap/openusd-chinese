原文摘录 / English Source Excerpts

An intrinsic light that emits light outwards from a cylinder. The cylinder is centered at the origin and has its major axis on the X axis. Note that the cylinder does not emit light from the flat end-caps.

Use CylinderLights to simulate tube-shaped fluorescent lights or similarly-shaped light sources, linear lights, light panels, and commercial lighting used in building interiors.

The following simple example has a CylinderLight positioned near a Sphere and Cube, with a length of 3 and a radius of 0.25. The light has been rotated and the light cone angle adjusted to limit the light spread, resulting in a thin band of light.
