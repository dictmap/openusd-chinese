逐段双语理解 / Paragraph-Level Bilingual Coverage

`UsdPhysicsJoint` 解决的问题是：在 USD scene description 中 author 一个用于约束 rigid bodies 运动的 physics schema prim。官方说明中的 “A joint constrains the movement of rigid bodies” 应按 schema 声明来读，它描述的是可交换、可组合的 USD 数据模型；真正的约束求解、稳定性、断裂事件和 articulation 初始化仍由下游 physics runtime 或导入器实现。

`UsdPhysicsJoint` authors a physics schema prim that constrains rigid-body motion in USD scene description. The official phrase “A joint constrains the movement of rigid bodies” describes the USD authoring schema, while actual constraint solving, stability, break events, and articulation initialization remain runtime responsibilities.

该 joint 可以连接两个 rigid bodies，也可以连接一个 rigid body 与 world。`CreateBody0Rel()` 和 `CreateBody1Rel()` author 的是 relationship targets，而不是把 body 对象复制进 joint。调试时应检查两个 relationship 是否指向带有相应 rigid-body schema/API 的 prim；如果只设置一端，另一端通常按 world 参考解释，但具体如何落到求解器坐标系需要看物理后端。

The joint can connect two rigid bodies, or one rigid body and the world. `CreateBody0Rel()` and `CreateBody1Rel()` author relationship targets instead of embedding body objects into the joint. If only one side is authored, the other side is normally interpreted against the world, with final coordinate handling delegated to the physics backend.

官方摘录说默认 joint primitive 定义 D6 joint，三个 linear 和三个 angular degrees of freedom 默认都是 free。这里的 D6 不是说已经限制了六个自由度，而是说 joint schema 的基础形态可以表达三轴线性与三轴角向自由度；具体锁定、限制或驱动通常由派生 schema 或附加属性组合表达，例如 `UsdPhysicsPrismaticJoint`、`UsdPhysicsRevoluteJoint`、`UsdPhysicsSphericalJoint` 等相邻类型。

The default primitive defines a D6 joint where three linear and three angular degrees of freedom are initially free. D6 means the schema can represent three translational and three angular degrees of freedom; locking, limiting, or driving those degrees usually comes from derived schemas or additional authored properties.

`CreateLocalPos0Attr()`、`CreateLocalRot0Attr()`、`CreateLocalPos1Attr()` 和 `CreateLocalRot1Attr()` 是本页最容易误读的一组属性。它们描述 joint frame 在 body0/body1 各自局部空间中的位置和旋转，不是世界空间坐标，也不是 prim 的 `xformOp`。如果两个局部 frame 没有对齐，求解器可能在加载时产生初始误差、拉伸、抖动或自动校正；排查机器人/机械结构时应优先核对这四个属性和 body 的实际 transform。

`CreateLocalPos0Attr()`, `CreateLocalRot0Attr()`, `CreateLocalPos1Attr()`, and `CreateLocalRot1Attr()` describe the joint frame in each body’s local space. They are not world-space coordinates and are not `xformOp` transforms. Misaligned local frames often explain initial constraint error, stretching, jitter, or runtime correction.

`CreateJointEnabledAttr()` author joint 的启用状态。它控制这个 joint schema 是否应参与求解，但不等同于删除 prim，也不表示 relationship targets 或局部 frame 无效。工具链中常见做法是保留完整 joint 数据，通过 `jointEnabled` 临时关闭约束，以便调试装配层次、比较自由运动和受约束运动。

`CreateJointEnabledAttr()` authors whether the joint should participate in solving. It does not delete the prim and does not invalidate relationships or local-frame data. Tools often keep the full joint data and temporarily disable the joint for assembly or simulation debugging.

`CreateCollisionEnabledAttr()` 控制 jointed bodies 之间是否允许 collision。官方摘录提醒默认行为会禁用相连 bodies 之间的 collision，这一点对机械铰链、门、连杆和机器人关节很重要：不希望相邻部件因为接触面重叠而自碰撞爆炸时通常保持禁用；若需要真实接触限位，则必须明确启用碰撞并同时处理碰撞形状、间隙和求解器稳定性。

`CreateCollisionEnabledAttr()` controls collision between jointed bodies. The official default disables collision between connected bodies, which is important for hinges, doors, links, and robot joints where adjacent parts may otherwise self-collide at their interface.

`CreateBreakForceAttr()` 与 `CreateBreakTorqueAttr()` author 断裂阈值。它们表达的是 USD physics schema 中的 break force / break torque 数据，不保证所有 runtime 都会以同样方式产生断裂事件，也不替代碰撞、质量、惯性和 timestep 的稳定性设置。阅读本页时应把“author 了阈值”和“求解器实际断开 joint”分开验证。

`CreateBreakForceAttr()` and `CreateBreakTorqueAttr()` author break thresholds. They store USD physics schema data and do not guarantee identical break-event behavior across all runtimes; authored threshold and solver breakage should be validated separately.

`CreateExcludeFromArticulationAttr()` 表达 joint 是否从 articulation 构建中排除。这个属性位于 USD 语义层，用来告诉导入器或 runtime 这个 joint 不应参与某些 articulation 组织；它不自动重新组织场景层级，也不保证每个物理后端都支持相同的 articulation 切分策略。对机器人和复杂机械装配而言，这个属性应与 rigid body API、关节树拓扑和运行时导入日志一起检查。

`CreateExcludeFromArticulationAttr()` indicates whether the joint should be excluded from articulation construction. It is a USD-level semantic hint and should be checked together with rigid-body APIs, joint-tree topology, and runtime importer diagnostics.

`Define()` 与 `Get()` 是 schema wrapper 的入口：`Define()` 会在指定 `UsdStage` 和 `SdfPath` 上确保 prim 存在并应用对应 schema，`Get()` 则尝试从已有 stage/path 获取 wrapper。它们不会自动创建 body targets，不会补齐 local frame，也不会检查 D6 自由度是否符合某个机械设计。`UsdPhysicsJoint` 和 `UsdPrim`、`SdfPath`、`UsdSchemaRegistry` 的关系应按 OpenUSD 常规 typed schema 模式理解。

`Define()` and `Get()` are schema-wrapper entry points. `Define()` ensures a schema prim at a `UsdStage`/`SdfPath`, while `Get()` wraps an existing prim. They do not author body targets, fill local frames, or validate mechanical intent automatically.

所有 `Create*Attr()` 与 `Create*Rel()` 方法都遵循 OpenUSD “Create vs Get” 惯例：Create 方法负责 author spec 或返回可 author 的 property handle，Get 方法读取已存在的 property handle。它们不代表数值已经具备物理一致性。真正的完整检查应覆盖 relationship target 存在性、body schema 应用、local frame 对齐、质量和惯性、collision 设置、articulation 参与方式，以及目标 runtime 对 `UsdPhysicsJoint` 的支持程度。

All `Create*Attr()` and `Create*Rel()` methods follow the OpenUSD Create-vs-Get property idiom. They author or return property handles, but do not prove physical consistency. A complete validation also checks targets, body schemas, local frames, mass/inertia, collision settings, articulation participation, and runtime support.

常见误读包括：把 `localPos0`/`localRot0` 当作世界坐标；以为默认 D6 joint 已经锁住某个自由度；以为 author `breakForce` 就一定会断裂；以为 `collisionEnabled=false` 是场景碰撞全局关闭；或以为 `jointEnabled=false` 会删除 joint。正确理解是：`UsdPhysicsJoint` 提供可组合的 USD 关节描述，仿真结果必须结合物理后端和完整刚体配置复验。

Common misreads include treating local frames as world transforms, assuming the default D6 joint locks degrees of freedom, assuming authored break thresholds always break, interpreting `collisionEnabled=false` globally, or expecting `jointEnabled=false` to delete the joint. The correct reading is that `UsdPhysicsJoint` is a composable USD joint description whose simulation behavior must be validated with the runtime.
